What we can help with

  • Payment & refund issues — receipt missing, duplicate charge, refund status, refund eligibility under our Refund Policy. Always include your order reference from the receipt email.
  • Access issues — locked out, missing content, video playback problems. Include the email you enrolled with.
  • Privacy & data requests — export, correction, or deletion of your data under our Privacy Policy. Email from the address associated with your enrollment so we can verify.
  • Course content questions — clarifications on lessons, references for the citations we discuss, suggestions for future modules.
  • Legal notices — DMCA, regulatory inquiries, formal complaints. Use the mailing address above or send to support@ with a subject line beginning [LEGAL].
Before filing a chargeback — please email us first. If your request qualifies under our Refund Policy, we will issue the refund. Chargebacks impose processing fees on us that legitimate refunds do not, and they suspend your access pending resolution.
