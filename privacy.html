8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of what we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your information, subject to legal retention requirements (Section 6).
  • Restriction — request that we restrict processing in specific circumstances.
  • Portability — request a structured machine-readable export.
  • Objection — object to processing on grounds of legitimate interest.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing relies on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint — with the data protection authority in your country (e.g., the ICO in the UK, your state attorney general in the US, your DPA in the EU/EEA).

To exercise any of these rights, email support@1hour100k.xyz from the address associated with your enrollment. We respond within 30 days. We do not charge a fee for reasonable requests.

9. California (CCPA) / Colorado / Virginia / similar state rights

If you are a resident of a US state with a comprehensive privacy law, you have rights as enumerated in Section 8. Additionally:

  • We do not "sell" or "share" your personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA. We have not done so in the past 12 months.
  • We do not use sensitive personal information (as defined by these laws) for any purpose other than what is strictly necessary to provide the Course.
  • You may designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf, in which case we will verify the agent's authority before responding.

10. Cookies and local storage

This site does not set any cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. We use the browser's localStorage to remember a small amount of state between visits (specifically, the return URL stashed when you arrive from a partner system, used so the post-payment confirmation page can return you to where you came from). You can clear this at any time through your browser's storage settings; doing so will not affect your Course access, only the post-checkout redirect convenience.

Square's checkout page itself may set cookies necessary for payment processing — see Square's privacy notice linked above for details.

11. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include TLS in transit (enforced on every endpoint), least-privilege access controls, and minimization of stored data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; you are responsible for keeping your account email and any credentials confidential.

12. Children

The Course is intended for adults 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a person under 18 has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.
